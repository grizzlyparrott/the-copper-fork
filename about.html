About Us

Our story and philosophy

Our Story

Founded in 2025 in Bullhead City, Arizona, The Copper Fork was born from a passion for honest cooking and a desire to bring a modern American bistro to our community. What started as a family dream has blossomed into a vibrant restaurant where locals and travellers alike gather to share memorable meals.

We chose our name to reflect the beauty of the Southwest: copper represents Arizona’s rich mining heritage, while the fork celebrates our dedication to great food. Our team works tirelessly to create dishes that balance comfort with creativity.

Our Philosophy

We believe that food should be as fresh as it is flavourful. That’s why we partner with local farmers, butchers and bakers to source seasonal ingredients. We cook from scratch – from house‑made sauces to desserts – and avoid shortcuts that compromise quality.

At The Copper Fork we strive to create a warm, inviting atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. Whether you’re celebrating a milestone or just popping in for lunch, our goal is to make you feel at home while enjoying a memorable meal.
